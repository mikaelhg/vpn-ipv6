    What and why

    The objective is to set up a IPv6-only "corporate intranet" type VPN, with services such as secure DNS and NAT64.

    IPv6 because I've seen various disasters relating to IPv4 corporate networks operating in the 10.x.x.x and 192.168.x.x spaces.

    Network space

    Private IPv6 space, RFC-4193.

    Local development

    Vagrant, Virtualbox, Ansible.

    Vagrant and Virtualbox manage local VMs, Ansible provisions them.

    Boxes:

    • ubuntu/focal64
    • gusztavvargadr/windows-10

    VPN tools

    WireGuard, ShadowSocks, TAYGA.

    WireGuard connects VPN clients and VPN servers.

    ShadowSocks provides a TCP 443 connection for WireGuard.

    TAYGA provides NAT64 services for connections from the VPN to the outside world.
